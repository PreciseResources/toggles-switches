Custom Events

  1. Add .active to body on mouseover

    data-switch-on="body" data-switch-event="mouseover"
    Mouseover

  2. Remove .active from body on mouseout

    data-switch-off="body" data-switch-event="mouseout"
    Mouseout

  3. Toggle .active on body on mouseover/out

    data-toggle="body" data-toggle-event="mouseover,mouseout"
    Hover on/off

  4. Add .active and remove .inactive from body on keyup

    data-switch-replace="body" data-switch-add="active" data-switch-remove="inactive" data-switch-event="keyup"

  5. Toggle between .active and .inactive on body on change

    data-toggle-replace="body" data-toggle-add="active" data-toggle-remove="active" data-switch-event="change"
